Wyngz can be a sweet heart of a cat when she has her charm mojo working. She loves to snuggle
and spend time watching T.V. in the same room as her human care takers and she is quite low
maintenance. Many cats have the reputation as being loners or non interested in humans though
Wyngz is very much the opposite of that. She responds to her name and other nick names we have for
her including Wendy, Little Little, Wild Wyngz and Ki Ki Wyngz(short for kitty, kitty, Wyngz).
Our family consists of me and my mother and Wyngz genuinely fits in perfectly. My mother likes
to pet Wyngz and Wyngz does not mind a pet, even if it is from someone who isn't all cutesy with
animals. I am more the owner of Wyngz and I constantly pick her up and am petting her so naturally
she is more attached to me. My mother remarks that Wyngz is just like a little puppy because Wyngz
follows me around the house and comes when I call her. When we first got Wyngz she was scared the
first few days but she opened up quite quickly. When she was younger, this being last year, we
had our house tented for termites so we took Wyngz with us on a small vacation to Newport beach.
It was only a matter of minutes before she took to the Air BnB we had rented. She was walking
around peeking out all the windows and seemed to be enjoying the vacation as much as we did!
Wyngz is really a great pet and I am grateful we were able to rescue her from off the streets
and treat her to the good life of a spoiled indoor cat.
